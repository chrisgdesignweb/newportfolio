About Chris

I'm a Product Designer with an interest in how design and technology intertwine to solve problems for everyday users. I enjoy working in collaborative environments with design and development to execute and implement these solutions. I have over 10 years experience working with all levels of organizations, from Startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Over the past few years I have dedicated my time to help educate the next wave of UX Designers through Bloc and Thinkful. Teaching others design process/techniques has been satisfying and a great way to give back to this industry.

I live on beautiful Cape Cod with my lovely wife Jessica and two rescue dogs Danny & Barnum.
