About Chris

I'm a Product Designer with a passion for how design and technology intertwine to solve problems for everyday users. I enjoy working in collaborative environments with design and development teams to execute and implement these solutions. I have over 10 years experience working with large and small organizations.

Over the past few years I have dedicated my time to help educate the next wave of UX Designers through Bloc and Thinkful. Teaching others design process and techniques has been extremely satisfying and a great way to give back to this industry.

I live on beautiful Cape Cod with my lovely wife Jessica and two rescue dogs Danny & Barnum.
